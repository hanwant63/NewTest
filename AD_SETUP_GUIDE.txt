═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
📝 STEP 1: SIGN UP FOR MONETAG
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to: https://monetag.com
2. Click "Sign Up" (top right)
3. Fill in your details:
   - Email
   - Password
   - Choose "Publisher" as account type
4. Verify your email
5. Login to your dashboard

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🎬 STEP 2: CREATE VIDEO AD ZONE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. In Monetag dashboard, go to "Zones" or "Create Zone"
2. Select ad format: "Direct Link" or "SmartLink"
3. Configure:
   - Website/Traffic Source: "Telegram Bot"
   - Country: "India" (or your target country)
   - Traffic Type: "Mobile"
4. Create Zone
5. Copy your ZONE ID (looks like: 123456 or ABC123)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
⚙️ STEP 3: CONFIGURE YOUR BOT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. In Replit Secrets, add:
   Key: AD_ID
   Value: <your zone ID from step 2>

2. Click "Add new secret" button in Replit
3. Restart your bot

NOTE: Works with ANY ad network! Just use AD_ID for any service:
- Monetag zone ID
- Adsterra zone ID  
- AdMaven zone ID
- Any other ad network ID

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
⏰ HOW TO CHANGE PREMIUM DURATION (Hours)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📂 File: ad_monetization.py
📍 Line: 7

Change this line:
   PREMIUM_DURATION_HOURS = 3

To whatever you want:
   PREMIUM_DURATION_HOURS = 1    (1 hour)
   PREMIUM_DURATION_HOURS = 6    (6 hours)
   PREMIUM_DURATION_HOURS = 24   (24 hours/1 day)

After changing, restart your bot workflow.
