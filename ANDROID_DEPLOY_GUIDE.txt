================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING FROM ANDROID
================================================================================

Problem: Bot not responding
Solution:
systemctl status telegram-bot.service
journalctl -u telegram-bot.service -n 50

Problem: Need to change credentials
Solution:
cd /root/telegram-bot
nano .env
(Edit the file, then Ctrl+X, Y, Enter)
systemctl restart telegram-bot.service

Problem: Bot crashed
Solution:
systemctl restart telegram-bot.service
(Systemd will auto-restart, but you can manually restart too)

Problem: Lost connection to VPS
Solution:
Just reconnect using JuiceSSH or Termux
Your bot keeps running even when you disconnect!

Problem: Forgot VPS password
Solution:
Check your VPS provider's website, they usually have a password reset option
